Snow Leopard

Type: Big Cat

Lives in: Mountain ranges of Central Asia

Diet: Deer, goats, and wild sheep

Cool Fact: Can leap up to 15 metres in one jump!

Duck

Type: Bird (waterfowl)

Lives in: Ponds, lakes, rivers, wetlands, and marshes.

Diet: plants, seeds, insects, worms, snails, and small fish.

Cool Fact: Ducks have waterproof feathers that help keep them dry while swimming.
